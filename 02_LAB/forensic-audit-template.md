# ARCHES Startup Forensic Audit: 12-Pillar Readiness Framework
**Objective:** To certify deep-tech SMEs for the 2026–2030 Brussels-Ankara Innovation Corridor.

---

## SECTION 1: TECHNICAL & IP PROVENANCE (The Foundation)
* **Pillar 01: IP Cleanliness & Freedom to Operate (FTO):** Verification of patent filings, open-source license compliance, and university spin-off agreements.
* **Pillar 02: Technical Debt Assessment:** Auditing the codebase and hardware architecture for scalability issues that hinder EU-level integration.
* **Pillar 03: R&D Sovereignty:** Evaluating the reliance on non-EU/non-TR critical components (supply chain resilience).

## SECTION 2: REGULATORY & ETHICAL ALIGNMENT (The Bridge)
* **Pillar 04: EU AI Act Compliance:** Classification of risk levels and verification of algorithmic transparency.
* **Pillar 05: Data Sovereignty (GDPR/KVKK):** Forensic review of data processing, storage, and cross-border transfer protocols.
* **Pillar 06: Green Deal Taxonomy:** Assessment of the startup’s "Environmental Footprint" and alignment with circular economy mandates.

## SECTION 3: MARKET & DIGITAL AUTHORITY (The Velocity)
* **Pillar 07: Digital Discoverability (AI SEO):** Leveraging high-velocity SEO infrastructure to ensure the startup dominates niche technical keywords in the EU market.
* **Pillar 08: GTM Narrative Fit:** Does the startup’s value proposition solve a "Strategic Autonomy" problem for the European Commission?
* **Pillar 09: Value Chain Integration:** Identification of Tier 1 European industrial partners for pilot projects.

## SECTION 4: ORGANIZATIONAL DNA (The Human Layer)
* **Pillar 10: Talent Density & Scaling Latency:** Analysis of the core team's ability to transition from "Founder-led" to "Institutional-led."
* **Pillar 11: Financial Forensic Review:** Auditing past grants (TÜBİTAK, etc.) to ensure no "administrative ghosts" hinder EU VC due diligence.
* **Pillar 12: Human-Centric Design Impact:** Measuring the Social Development Goal (SDG) alignment of the core technology.

---
**Audit Logic:** Each pillar is scored 1–10. A total score >85 qualifies for the **ARCHES "Sovereign-Ready" Seal.**
